## One Page

### The Problem

Privacy compliance is now a baseline requirement for any online business. GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and a wave of new state laws require you to know what personal data you collect, tell people about it, and give them control over it. The penalties for non-compliance are real — up to €20 million under GDPR and $7,500 per violation under CCPA.

But if you're a small business — a SaaS startup, an e-commerce store, a digital agency — the tools built to solve this problem weren't built for you.

On one end, cookie banner tools like CookieBot and Termly offer a popup and a template policy. They don't actually scan your site in depth, they can't tell you if you're compliant, and they leave you guessing about everything else — data mapping, DSARs, vendor management, privacy impact assessments.

On the other end, enterprise platforms like OneTrust, Transcend, and TrustArc deliver comprehensive compliance — but at $5,000 to $50,000+ per year, with lengthy implementations that assume you have a dedicated privacy team to run them.

There's nothing in the middle. Until now.

### The Solution

Custodia is the AI-native privacy compliance platform built for small and mid-sized businesses.

It starts with a scan. Our headless browser crawls your website exactly like a real visitor, detecting every cookie, tracking pixel, third-party script, fingerprinting technique, and local storage item. AI classifies each tracker by purpose and maps where your visitors' data is being sent — which vendors, which countries, under which legal frameworks.

From that scan, Custodia automatically generates your full compliance stack:

- **Consent banner** tailored to your actual trackers, with jurisdiction-aware logic that applies GDPR opt-in for EU visitors and CCPA opt-out for Californians — including Google Consent Mode v2 support.
- **Privacy policy and cookie policy** written from real data, not a fill-in-the-blank template. When your site changes, your policy updates automatically.
- **Compliance dashboard** showing your status across every applicable regulation — green, yellow, or red — with specific, plain-English recommendations to close any gaps.
- **Weekly re-scans** that catch new trackers, broken consent flows, or regulatory changes before they become violations.
- **DSAR management** to handle access, deletion, and portability requests with deadline tracking and AI-drafted responses.
- **Data governance tools** including data inventory, vendor registry, data flow visualization, and privacy impact assessments.
