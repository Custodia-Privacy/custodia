## Features Section

**Section Headline:**
Everything you need to stay on the right side of privacy law.

### Feature 1: AI Website Scanner & Data Mapping

**Headline:** See exactly what your website collects.

**Body:** Custodia's headless browser crawls every page, detecting cookies, tracking pixels, third-party scripts, local storage, and fingerprinting techniques. AI classifies each one by purpose — analytics, advertising, functional — and maps where your visitors' data actually goes. No more guessing what that Meta pixel is sending back to Facebook.

### Feature 2: Smart Consent Management

**Headline:** A consent banner that actually matches your site.

**Body:** Most consent banners are generic templates. Custodia generates yours from real scan data, so every category listed is something your site actually uses. It auto-detects visitor location and applies the right legal framework — GDPR opt-in for EU visitors, CCPA opt-out for Californians — with full Google Consent Mode v2 support. Consent receipts are stored for audit-ready proof.

### Feature 3: AI Privacy Policy & Legal Documents

**Headline:** Privacy policies that write and update themselves.

**Body:** Custodia generates your privacy policy, cookie policy, and terms of service based on what the scanner found — not a fill-in-the-blank template. When your site changes, your documents update automatically. Each section comes with a plain-English explanation alongside the legal text, so you actually understand what you're publishing.

### Feature 4: Compliance Dashboard & Monitoring

**Headline:** One screen. Every regulation. Always current.

**Body:** See your compliance status across GDPR, CCPA, CPRA, and emerging state laws at a glance. Green means compliant. Yellow means action needed. Red means fix it now. Every issue comes with a specific, plain-English recommendation. Weekly re-scans catch drift before regulators do.

### Feature 5: Ongoing Monitoring & Alerts

**Headline:** Compliance isn't a one-time thing. Neither are we.

**Body:** New marketing tool added a tracking pixel? Developer pushed a script without consent coverage? Custodia catches it on the next scan and alerts you instantly — with a clear explanation of the issue and what to do about it. Your compliance posture stays current without manual audits.

### Feature 6: DSAR Management

**Headline:** Handle data requests without the legal overhead.

**Body:** When someone requests access to their data, asks you to delete it, or wants to know what you have — Custodia manages the entire workflow. Branded intake portal, automatic request classification, deadline tracking for GDPR's 30-day and CCPA's 45-day windows, and AI-drafted response letters. Privacy rights management on autopilot.

### Feature 7: Privacy Impact Assessments

**Headline:** Risk assessments in plain English, not legalese.

**Body:** Answer straightforward questions about your data practices. Custodia's AI generates formal assessment documents — GDPR Article 35 DPIAs, CCPA risk assessments — identifying high-risk processing and recommending mitigations. Export as PDF for regulators, investors, or your own records.

### Feature 8: Data Governance & Inventory

**Headline:** Know what data you have and where it lives.

**Body:** Catalog what personal data you collect, where it's stored, who has access, and how long you keep it. Track vendors and sub-processors with risk scoring. Visualize data flows across your systems. Set retention policies and get reminders when data should be purged.

## FAQ Section

**Section Headline:**
Questions? Answered.

**Q: What privacy regulations does Custodia cover?**
A: Custodia covers GDPR (EU), CCPA/CPRA (California), and the growing list of U.S. state privacy laws including Virginia (VCDPA), Colorado (CPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), and more. Our AI stays current with regulatory changes so you don't have to.

**Q: How does the website scanner work?**
A: We use a headless browser to visit your site exactly like a real user would. It loads every page, clicks through consent flows, and detects all cookies, tracking pixels, third-party scripts, local storage, and fingerprinting techniques. The AI then classifies each one and maps where data is being sent.

**Q: Is the auto-generated privacy policy legally valid?**
A: Custodia's policies are generated based on real data about what your site collects, using frameworks aligned with GDPR, CCPA, and other regulations. They're comprehensive and production-ready. That said, we always recommend having a lawyer review any legal document for your specific situation. The difference is: with Custodia, you're handing your lawyer a 95%-finished document instead of starting from scratch.

**Q: How is Custodia different from a cookie banner tool like CookieBot?**
A: Cookie banner tools give you a popup and a generic policy template. Custodia gives you the full compliance stack: scanning, consent management, policy generation, compliance monitoring, DSAR handling, and data governance — all informed by AI that actually knows what your site does. It's the difference between a smoke detector and a full fire safety system.

**Q: How is Custodia different from enterprise tools like OneTrust?**
A: OneTrust, Transcend, and TrustArc are built for Fortune 500 companies with dedicated privacy teams and six-figure budgets. They're powerful but massively overbuilt for small businesses. Custodia gives you the same core capabilities — scanning, consent, policies, monitoring, DSARs — at a price that makes sense, with AI doing the heavy lifting instead of requiring a team of specialists.

**Q: Do I really need this? My business is small.**
A: If you have a website that collects any personal data — even just analytics or a contact form — privacy regulations likely apply to you. GDPR applies if any EU residents visit your site. CCPA applies to many California-facing businesses. Fines start at €20 million for GDPR and $7,500 per violation for CCPA. The risk is real, even for small businesses. Custodia makes compliance achievable without a legal budget.

**Q: How long does setup take?**
A: Your first scan takes about 60 seconds. From there, deploying a consent banner and publishing a privacy policy takes roughly 15 minutes. Full compliance dashboard setup — including monitoring and alerts — is typically done within an hour.

**Q: Can I use Custodia for multiple websites?**
A: Yes. The Starter plan covers 1 site, Growth covers 3, and Business covers 10. Each site gets its own scan, consent banner, privacy policy, and compliance dashboard. Agency and enterprise plans for more sites are available — contact us.
