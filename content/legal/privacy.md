# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-04-22  
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-22

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Custodia ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website custodia-privacy.com (the "Site"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our Site, we automatically collect certain information through third-party services:

**Google Analytics Data:**
- Page views and website navigation patterns
- Session duration and bounce rates
- Device and browser information
- General geographic location (city/region level)
- Referral sources
- User demographics and interests (when available)

**reCAPTCHA Data:**
- Browser fingerprint information
- Device characteristics
- IP address
- Mouse movements and typing patterns
- Other behavioral data to distinguish humans from bots

**Server Log Information:**
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Date and time of access
- Pages requested

### Information You Provide

Currently, our Site does not include forms that collect personal information directly from users.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Website Analytics:** To understand how visitors use our Site, improve user experience, and optimize Site performance
- **Security:** To protect our Site from spam, abuse, and automated attacks through reCAPTCHA
- **Legal Compliance:** To comply with applicable laws and regulations
- **Technical Operations:** To maintain and troubleshoot our Site

## 3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not currently use essential or non-essential cookies on our Site.

### Third-Party Tracking

Our Site uses the following third-party services that may collect information through tracking technologies:

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### Rights Under GDPR (EU/UK Residents)

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have the following rights:

- **Access:** Request a copy of personal data we hold about you
- **Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate personal data
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your personal data
- **Restriction:** Request restriction of processing of your personal data
- **Portability:** Request transfer of your personal data
- **Objection:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

**Legal Basis for Processing:** We process your data based on legitimate interests for analytics and security purposes.

### Rights Under CCPA/CPRA (California Residents)

California residents have the following rights:

- **Know:** Request information about personal information collected, used, disclosed, or sold
- **Delete:** Request deletion of personal information
- **Correct:** Request correction of inaccurate personal information
- **Opt-Out:** Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information
- **Limit Use:** Limit use and disclosure of sensitive personal information
- **Non-Discrimination:** Not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights

**Note:** We do not sell or share personal information as defined by CCPA.

### Rights Under Virginia (VCDPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), and Colorado (CPA)

If you are a resident of Virginia, Connecticut, or Colorado, you have rights similar to GDPR including:

- Access to personal data
- Correction of personal data
- Deletion of personal data
- Portability of personal data
- Opt-out of targeted advertising, sale, or profiling

### How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@custodia-privacy.com. We will respond to your request within the timeframe required by applicable law.

For EU/UK residents, you may also contact our Data Protection Officer at Custodia Privacy Team.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain automatically collected information as follows:

- **Google Analytics data:** Retained according to Google's retention settings (typically 26 months)
- **Server logs:** Retained for up to 12 months for security and troubleshooting purposes
- **reCAPTCHA data:** Retained according to Google's data retention policies

## 7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

- Secure transmission protocols (HTTPS)
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls and authentication
- Data minimization practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete such information promptly.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Information collected through Google Analytics and reCAPTCHA may be transferred to and processed in the United States and other countries where Google operates. These transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards, including Google's compliance with applicable data protection frameworks.
