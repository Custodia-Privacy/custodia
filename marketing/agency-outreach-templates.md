# Agency Outreach Templates

Web agencies and digital consultants are a high-leverage segment: one agency sale = access to their full client roster. These templates are for cold outreach from the founder's email.

Target: Founders/directors of web agencies, digital marketing agencies, and freelance consultants who build/manage client websites.

Find targets: LinkedIn (search "web agency founder", "digital agency director", "web consultant"), agency directories (Clutch, DesignRush, Sortlist).

## Where to Find Targets

**LinkedIn searches:**
- "web agency founder" + location filter (US, UK, EU)
- "digital agency director"
- "freelance web developer" with 500+ connections
- "web consultant" in "Business Development" or "Owner" role

**Directories:**
- Clutch.co — filter by agency size (2–10 people), web design/development
- DesignRush — similar
- Sortlist — EU-focused agencies

**Communities:**
- /r/webdev — post about compliance tools (don't pitch directly)
- Designer News
- Indie Hackers — find agencies in "What are you working on?" threads

**Volume target:** 20–30 personalized emails per week. Expect 2–5% response rate, so 1–2 conversations per week. Goal is 3–5 agency pilots that might each bring 3–10 client sites.
