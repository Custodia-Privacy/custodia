## Description

### The Problem
Small businesses face a compliance gap. Cookie banner tools give you a popup — that's not real compliance. Enterprise platforms like OneTrust deliver comprehensive privacy management — at $5,000 to $50,000+/year.

If you're a SaaS startup, e-commerce store, or agency with 5-50 people, neither option works.

### What Custodia Does

Custodia is the AI-native privacy compliance platform that fills this gap:

**1. AI-Powered Website Scanner**
Our headless browser crawls your site like a real visitor, detecting every cookie, tracking pixel, third-party script, and data transfer. No manual configuration — the scanner finds everything.

**2. Smart Consent Banner**
Generated from your actual scan data. Jurisdiction-aware: GDPR opt-in for EU visitors, CCPA opt-out for Californians. Google Consent Mode v2 built in.

**3. AI-Generated Privacy Policy**
Written from your real data practices — not a fill-in-the-blank template. Auto-updates when your site changes.

**4. Compliance Dashboard**
See your status across GDPR, CCPA, and state laws at a glance. Specific, actionable recommendations in plain English.

**5. Weekly Re-Scans**
Catch new trackers, broken consent flows, and policy gaps before they become violations.

**6. DSAR Management**
Handle data access, deletion, and portability requests with deadline tracking and AI-drafted responses.

**7. Data Governance**
Data inventory, vendor registry, data flow mapping, and privacy impact assessments.

### Pricing
- **Free**: Scan any website — see what it's collecting
- **Starter ($29/mo)**: 1 site, consent banner, privacy policy, dashboard, weekly scans
- **Growth ($79/mo)**: 3 sites + DSARs, preference management, PIA templates
- **Business ($149/mo)**: 10 sites + data governance, vendor registry, API access
